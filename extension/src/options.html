Choose a whole number from 45 to 259. This is the actual source count where Brain stops new exports. Brain enforces 259 as the hard maximum even if your NotebookLM plan supports more; in a 300-source notebook, that leaves at least 41 sources outside Brain's export allowance.

Privacy boundary: the extension requests access only to notebooklm.google.com after you approve it. It does not request cookie, debugger, page-scripting, traffic-inspection, or all-sites permissions. It never stores Google session material or sends notebook/source IDs to Brain.
Disconnect safely: cancel or stop unresolved exports, then disconnect the connector in Brain first. The emergency local-clear control cannot revoke the server connector and can strand queued, uncertain, or processing work if used early.
