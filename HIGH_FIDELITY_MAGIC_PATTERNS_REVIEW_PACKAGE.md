## Required Review Coverage

Web:

- Login, unlock, setup PIN, and device pairing.
- Library browse, search, filters, multi-select, and Ask selected.
- Needs Upgrade queue.
- Capture and capture result states.
- Item detail variants for article, transcript, PDF, note, weak metadata-only, preview-only, and enriching states.
- Item detail right rail with Source and capture, Tags, Included topics, Collections, related items, and item actions.
- Ask with new conversation, selected conversation, source evidence, weak-source warning, collapsed global nav, expanded Ask history, and collapsed Ask history.
- Settings categories.

Android:

- Login, unlock, PIN keypad, first-time pairing, pairing code, pairing success, unreachable, session expired, and offline fallback.
- Library browse, search, filters, selected-items mode, and Needs Upgrade list.
- Share capture result and capture via bottom nav or floating action.
- Capture result states.
- Weak repair flow.
- Item detail variants and tabs: Original, Digest, Ask, Related, Details.
- Details tab with Source and capture, Tags, Included topics, Collections, offline/searchable state, and item actions.
- Ask default, scoped Ask, history sheet, conversation detail, citation sheet, and item Ask history.
- More/settings and offline/server-unreachable state.

## Known Product Rules To Preserve

- Source platform and Captured via are different fields.
- Tags, Included topics, and Collections are separate cards.
- Included topics are AI-detected and clickable, with no Add action.
- Android Library should not have a top rectangular capture button.
- Android should not show Continue Reading until reading-position tracking is designed.
- Web has two independent collapsible navigation layers on Ask: global nav and Ask history sub-navigation.
- Android Ask history uses a bottom sheet.
