## Traceability conclusion

The strongest trace is the lifecycle model: user problem → Inbox-first requirement → PRD workflow/archive/metrics acceptance criteria → append-only versioned technical plan. The weakest trace is prototype trust evidence: batch, mutation failure, conflict, deletion/topic change, unsaved-note safety, exact count semantics, and extended accessibility/responsive proof.

This v1 matrix should not be copied unchanged into final handoff. The coordinator must add v2 and adversarial-review links, record every QA finding disposition, replace gaps with final evidence or explicit blockers, and verify wiki/PR/commit links after publication.
