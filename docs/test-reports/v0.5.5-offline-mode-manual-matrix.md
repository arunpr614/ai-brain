## How to use this document

- 24 scenarios in 7 buckets. **Don't try to do them all in one session** — bucket A (basic happy path) covers 80% of the value in 10 min.
- **Each scenario has a verdict cell:** mark ✅ PASS / ❌ FAIL / ⏭ SKIP / ⚠️ PARTIAL.
- For ❌ FAIL or ⚠️ PARTIAL, fill in the **Evidence** field with what you saw + any logcat output. That's the unblocker for the bug fix.
- For ✅ PASS, the Evidence field is optional — a checkmark is fine.
- The §9 ship-readiness checklist at the bottom needs **A1, A2, A3, B1, B2, C1, F1 minimum** before the offline mode is considered shippable to main. The rest are nice-to-have.
- **Stop at the first ❌ — tell me before continuing.** Most failures are 30-second diagnoses when surfaced early; 30-minute hunts when papered over.
