The contrary case fails the current gates: Related usage/trust harm is unobserved, current source opening/citations and label-only cues are untested, note influence/consent behavior is unproven, and this council does not authorize a prototype or implementation.

## Consolidated assumptions and risks

- B-00 recruitment/evidence volume may be insufficient; the four-week stop rule must prevent indefinite deferral.
- Current runtime/provider/index state is not freshly verified and can confound baseline tasks.
- Five sessions/three episodes are directional, not population proof; recurrence across people or distinct weeks matters.
- Research must include accessible participation and avoid bias toward graph-interested/expert users.
- Privacy-safe outcome recording must exclude titles, URLs, note text, labels, paths, free text, screenshots, and raw library export.
- C-01 cannot turn origin/currentness into causal explanation; note-derived influence must disappear on consent withdrawal.
- C-02 cannot treat normalized similarity as permission to merge; read-only cues and mutations need separate decisions.
- C-03 inherits sensitive-association, false-authority, incomplete deletion/tombstone, scale, accessibility, ownership, and rollback risk even if implemented natively.

## Consolidated decision-changing validation

1. Run one bounded, consented, privacy-minimized discovery timebox with predeclared tasks, current/minimum comparators, material-improvement thresholds, accessibility sampling, retention/deletion owner, and forced exit.
2. Target at least five comparable sessions or three unprompted natural episodes across at least two participants or three distinct weeks for the actual single owner; treat this as directional evidence.
3. Compare current behavior, minimum change, and concept on the same task. Directional threshold proposed by the user-value perspective: at least 25% median task-time reduction or 20-point completion/correct-interpretation improvement, with no confidence, privacy, error, or accessibility regression. Freeze thresholds before exposure.
4. C-01: prove exact field/provenance/state mapping, faithful actual contribution for any full explanation, no provider egress, owner denial, stale/unknown/error behavior, immediate consent/deletion response, prohibited telemetry fields, access-mode parity, and cleanup.
5. C-02: test counts/sort/filter first; use adversarial label pairs; keep MVP read-only unless every mutation passes authorization, preview, origin preservation, atomicity, idempotency, retry/concurrency, partial-failure recovery, undo, audit, focus/status, and zero unrecoverable manual-intent loss.
6. C-03: first prove the family job and comparative advantage. Then select one variant and require exact relation allowlist/semantics, sensitive-relation policy, lifecycle/watermark/tombstone/rebuild/cancel/failure/cleanup, representative performance, privacy-safe metrics, exact dependency/SBOM/license/security plan, named maintainer, rollback drill, and equal text/keyboard/screen-reader/zoom/reflow/reduced-motion/mobile completion.
