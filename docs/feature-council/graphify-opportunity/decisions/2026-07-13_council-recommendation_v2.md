## B-00 disposition — conditional evidence action, not automatic authorization

B-00 is not a selected candidate and not a product Go. Its meaningful-advantage gate remains exactly:

**Fail (1/5), Unknown (4/5).**

No gate is waived. Current behavior/immediate no-go remains the default unless every B-00 start condition below is accepted in a separate decision.

### Step A — natural recurrence and healthy current baseline only

Step A may be separately authorized to answer whether a target problem occurs naturally and with sufficient consequence. It may not show, design, or test a candidate-specific concept or prototype.

Permitted evidence:

- unprompted natural-episode diary;
- current-surface observation after baseline readiness passes;
- consequence, severity, workaround, task failure, time, and confidence recorded without private content;
- current behavior only, not a candidate stimulus.

Prompted task sessions can characterize current usability. They cannot pass demonstrated-problem frequency.

### Required start conditions

Before Step A starts, a separate authorization must record all of:

1. **Owner:** AI Brain maintainer accepts research-record, recruitment/diary, privacy, accessibility, readout, and deletion responsibility.
2. **Evidence source:** recruitable participants or an accepted actual-single-owner diary plan.
3. **Value of information:** exact result that would change the current Defer/no-go disposition and why it is worth the delay.
4. **Protocol:** consent, withdrawal, minimization, access list, accessible participation, prohibited fields, publication review, retention, and deletion date.
5. **Baseline readiness:** dated app/build version, current feature availability/flags, provider readiness where relevant, index generation/readiness, representative fixture validity, and known degraded behavior.
6. **Dates:** fixed start and readout no later than 28 calendar days apart.
7. **Immediate-no-go fallback:** if any start condition fails, continue current behavior and do not start/extend discovery.

Degraded, stale, disabled, or confounded baseline sessions are invalidated and rescheduled; they are not scored.

### Natural recurrence gate

A target job is directionally recurring only after at least three **unprompted** episodes with recorded consequence/severity:

- across at least two participants; or
- across at least three distinct weeks for the actual single owner.

Five prompted sessions cannot substitute for this gate. The evidence is discovery-only and supports no population, adoption, retention, or durable-value claim.

### Step B — separately authorized concept comparison

Only after Step A passes the natural-recurrence gate may a new council decision authorize one smallest candidate-specific concept comparison. That authorization must name the artifact and boundary. It does not authorize implementation.

Step B is outside this final Defer decision. No concept, UX artifact, or interactive prototype is authorized now.

### No automatic extension

Step A ends at its fixed readout. Any additional evidence gap requires a new explicit council authorization and new deadline. There is no automatic or second extension.

## Directional measurement and hard guardrails

If Step B is later authorized, same-task current/minimum/concept evidence must use thresholds frozen before exposure. The Round 1 directional proposal—25% median task-time reduction or 20-point completion/correct-interpretation improvement—is exploratory only and cannot support statistical/population claims.

No average can offset any of these zero-tolerance failures:

- provenance/origin mislabeled;
- withdrawn note influence remains;
- private telemetry or prohibited content leaks;
- false causal/authoritative interpretation appears;
- accessibility blocks equivalent task completion;
- manual intent is unrecoverably lost;
- owner/authorization denial fails;
- deletion or feature-off cleanup leaves ineligible derived state.

## Minority opinions and residual risks

### Strongest contrary case

A minimal C-01 source-kind/current-state cue could be the cheapest learning instrument in an existing high-intent surface. A strictly read-only C-02 counts/cues proof is the secondary contrary case. Neither is authorized because natural demand, current/minimum advantage, faithful state, privacy/accessibility, and cleanup are unproven.

### Defer risk

Step A may yield too few episodes and delay a useful improvement. The start-value test, 28-day limit, and immediate-no-go fallback prevent an institutionalized defer loop.

### Rare but severe jobs

Directional recurrence may miss rare high-consequence work. Such evidence can be recorded as a minority risk but cannot silently lower the gate.
